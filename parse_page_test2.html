The Jibarito Stop just opened up a new restaurant a few blocks from my work, and I couldn't be more excited. Always on the lookout for new lunch places and eager to support new local businesses, myself and the rest of the folks in my office decided to order take out and give it a go. The reviews were unanimous... the food was delicious. Given that the name of the restaurant is the Jibarito Stop, I figured I had no choice but to try a Jibarito. I was a little skeptical on the combination of ingredients (not being a huge plantain fan), but figured "when in Rome!". I couldn't have been happier with my choice. I chose a Steak Jibarito, which comes with a big ol' serving of arroz con gandules (rice with pigeon peas). The steak was wonderfully seasoned, and the plantain "bun" gave it just the right flavor. Very happy to see such a great addition to the neighborhood and looking forward to trying some more items from the menu!
